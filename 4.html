Omega Go is an letter puzzle game that challenges your brain. It's a relaxing but addictive mobile game that you can play anywhere, anytime. Simply merge letters together to form higher letters; the higher the letter, the more points you earn!

Fun fact: The last Greek letter is Omega and the game play is similar to the classic board game Go, so we call it "Omega Go".
