TINY APPS

Established in: Jan 2017 ・ Position: Co-founder・ URL: tinyapps.design

The Short Story: I started Tiny Apps with James Ormond in my college senior year. We combined our specialties in design and developments, crafting indie games together. It was a garage project, but it turned out well. So far, we've launched three games: Omega Go, 123Tiles, and Letterix. We love making, playing, and talking about our games. Feel free to work hard and play hard with us!

Spoiler: The fourth game is about a bunch of squares.
